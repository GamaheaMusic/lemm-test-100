## ğŸ“Š Understanding Waveforms

### What the waveform shows:
- **Height**: Volume/amplitude
- **Density**: Frequency content
- **Progress bar**: Current playback position
- **Color**: Which component you're viewing

### Reading the waveform:
- **Tall peaks**: Loud sections (drums, transients)
- **Shorter sections**: Quieter passages
- **Dense/thick**: Rich harmonic content
- **Sparse/thin**: Minimal or pure tones
